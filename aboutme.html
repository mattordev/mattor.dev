About me


I'm Matthew Roberts, a 22 year old game developer, mainly specialising in programming and software development. I have been developing and playing with code, games, software and hardware for as long as I can remember.


It's always been of great interest to me how things work beyond the pretty interfaces and UI, when you pair that idea with my general love for games and technology you can see why I chose to persue this field. Over the years of learning and often teaching I have fostered my ability to be a hardworking indvidual who can apdapt to a multitude of different situations, never being detered by any challenge that seems tricky at first.


Over the years I've had many different types of hobbies ranging from metal detecting, making music and streaming all the way to something as far out as model rocketry and designing flight computers to control and guide said model rockets. Besides those, I've had a passion for gaming for as long as I can remember and have been playing them for over a decade. Besides those things, I love to explore the environments in the real world and try and promote healthy development by going on bike rides, walking trials and getting out as much as I can.
